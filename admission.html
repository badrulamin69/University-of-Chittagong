Eligibility Criteria

For Bachelor Programs

  • Minimum GPA 3.5 in SSC and HSC combined
  • Or minimum GPA 3.0 in both SSC and HSC separately
  • Must pass HSC or equivalent examination
  • Age limit: Maximum 22 years (general) or 25 years (quota)
  • Must appear in admission test

For Master Programs

  • Bachelor degree with minimum CGPA 2.5
  • Or minimum second class in bachelor degree
  • Relevant bachelor degree for the program
  • Must pass entrance examination
  • Interview may be required
